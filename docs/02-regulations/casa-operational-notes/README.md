## Use Status

CASA states these notes are a reproduction of a booklet originally published by the Civil Aviation Authority. CASA also states the notes are no longer in print, have not been edited, and may contain information relating to systems that are no longer in production or have been modernised.

Use this source as CASA-hosted reference material for NDB/ADF theory and cockpit technique. Do not treat it as current regulation, AIP procedure, or a replacement for current Part 91 MOS/AIP operational requirements.
