# PIFR-063

## Objective

MOS Schedule 3 Section 2.2 Unit 2.2.1 13.2.1: State visual approach procedures, day and night, in Class G airspace and at non-controlled aerodromes, including the following: (a) landing manoeuvres; (b) cancellation of SARWATCH; (c) operation of VHF aerodrome lighting (PAL).
