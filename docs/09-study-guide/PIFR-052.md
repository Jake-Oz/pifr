# PIFR-052

## Objective

MOS Schedule 3 Section 2.2 Unit 2.2.1 11.1.5: State the procedures for the following components of a flight profile for day and night operations in CTA and CTR: (a) departure; (b) climb; (c) transition to cruise (levelling out); (d) cruise; (e) change of levels; (f) descent and visual approach.
