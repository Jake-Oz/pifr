- Status: Mapped
- Exam importance: High
- Source status notes: Verified against current Part 91 MOS compilation 7 (F2026C00214) and AIP Book Amendment 126 current 19 MAR 2026. Suitability requires the nominated alternate to be suitable as a planned destination, not itself require an alternate, not be a helideck, and meet weather/navigation/lighting requirements after considering NOTAM and forecast information.
