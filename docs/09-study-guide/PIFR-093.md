# PIFR-093

## Objective

MOS Schedule 3 Section 2.2 Unit 2.2.1 17.1.5: Be aware of the human factors limitations associated with the use of GNSS equipment to provide safeguards against navigational errors and loss of situational awareness because of the following: (a) mode errors; (b) data entry errors; (c) data validation and checking, including independent cross-checking procedures; (d) automation induced complacency; (e) non-standardisation of the GNSS receiver units; (f) human information processing and situational awareness.
